Submit A Guest Post

Are you passionate about sports, gaming or entertainment? Do you have unique insights, analysis or perspectives to share? HJ Trending welcomes contributions from writers, analysts and experts worldwide.

Why Write For HJ Trending?

  • Large Audience: Reach millions of sports fans, gamers and entertainment enthusiasts.
  • Editorial Support: Our team works with you to refine and optimize your content.
  • Author Credit: Full byline with bio and links to your profiles and website.
  • SEO Benefits: Backlinks and author attribution boost your personal brand.
  • Network: Connect with other writers and industry professionals.

Topics We Accept

Sports: Football (soccer), cricket, basketball, tennis, esports at all levels.

Gaming: Game reviews, esports analysis, industry news, gaming hardware.

Entertainment: Movies, TV shows, celebrities, awards, entertainment industry news.

Trending: Breaking news, viral stories, cultural moments in sports and gaming.

Note: We do NOT accept articles on politics, religion, or content that violates our editorial guidelines.

Submission Guidelines

Article Requirements

  • Length: 1,000 - 3,000 words (flexible for special topics).
  • Format: Well-structured with clear H2 and H3 subheadings.
  • Originality: Must be original, unpublished content (first publication).
  • Quality: Well-researched, fact-checked, grammatically correct.
  • Images: High-quality images (1200x630px or larger) with proper attribution.
  • Tone: Conversational yet authoritative, matching HJ Trending's voice.

Author Bio Requirements

  • Author name and professional title
  • Brief author bio (50-100 words)
  • Author photo (headshot, 300x300px)
  • Links to your website, Twitter, LinkedIn or other relevant profiles
