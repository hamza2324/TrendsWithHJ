About HJ

HJ Trending is a daily news platform covering sports, gaming, entertainment and everything the world is talking about. No filler, no fluff just what's actually happening right now.

Our Mission

HJ Trending exists to deliver real news, honest analysis, and genuine trending stories without the clickbait, without the sensationalism, and without wasting your time. We believe the world deserves better coverage of sports, gaming, entertainment and everything happening online.

Who We Are

Founded by Hamza Jadoon, HJ Trending is built by people who are tired of fake news and empty analysis. We cover FIFA 2026, Premier League football, NBA, gaming releases, entertainment news, and viral moments all with one goal: to give you the truth, explained clearly and fast.

Based in Pakistan, our team writes daily for an audience that demands straight facts and real takes. No algorithm tricks. No manufactured outrage. Just what you need to know, when you need to know it.
