Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device. They help websites remember information about your visits, such as preferences, login status, and browsing behavior.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They remember your preferences, keep you logged in (if applicable), and enable site navigation. Without these cookies, some website features may not work.

  • Session cookies (temporary, deleted when you close your browser)
  • Security cookies (protect against fraud and attacks)
  • Preference cookies (remember your settings)

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. These cookies track:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Geographic location and device information
  • Traffic sources and user behavior patterns
  • Performance issues and user experience metrics

Google Analytics Privacy: Google may use this data according to their privacy policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser extension.

Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense uses cookies to display targeted advertisements based on your browsing history and interests. These cookies:

  • Track your visits across websites
  • Build a profile of your interests
  • Deliver personalized ads
  • Measure ad performance

Google AdSense Privacy: See Google's advertising policies for more details. You can manage ad preferences in your Google Account settings.

Social Media Cookies

Social media buttons and widgets (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn) may set cookies to understand how you interact with this content.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted partners to set cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

How To Control Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and site data
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy, Search and Services > Cookies and other site data

Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of specific services:

  • Google Analytics: Download the opt-out extension
  • Google AdSense: Manage preferences in your Google Account
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Preferences

Cookie Retention

Cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: May remain for weeks, months, or years depending on cookie settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Typically expire after 12-26 months
  • Advertising Cookies: Vary by provider; usually several months
