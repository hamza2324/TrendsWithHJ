What the Reset Means for the Future of the MCU

If the theory is correct ‚Äî and it now appears to be ‚Äî the post-Secret Wars MCU will be a fundamentally different creative landscape. Marvel Studios will have the ability to recast legacy roles without contradicting existing canon, introduce entirely new versions of characters, and start Phase 7 without the weight of 16 years of continuity obligations hanging over every story decision.

This is exactly what the MCU needs. The Infinity Saga built something extraordinary ‚Äî a 23-film narrative spanning a decade that culminated in Endgame becoming the second highest-grossing film in history. But everything after Endgame has struggled under the weight of continuity that became too complex to manage cleanly. Secret Wars, structured as a genuine ending and a genuine new beginning simultaneously, is the creative solution that allows Marvel to honour what was built while clearing the deck for the next generation.
